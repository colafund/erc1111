ERC-1111 is an EIP token standard proposed by PFPAsia, the team of the NFT project DAN. It allows tokens to exist in the form of FT or NFT. The form of existence is chosen by the holder. It is an experiment in NFT fragmentation.

The ERC-1111 protocol solves the problem of insufficient liquidity of traditional NFTs through the bidirectional conversion function between NFTs and FTs. Traditional NFTs can only be traded through secondary market orders. It cannot be traded at any time through a liquidity pool like a token. Buying and selling tokens in hand is undoubtedly a huge milestone for traditional NFTs.
