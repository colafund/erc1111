ERC1111

REDT Protocol.

PFPAsia Team.

ERC-1111 is an EIP token standard proposed by PFPAsia, the team of the NFT project DAN. It allows tokens to exist in the form of FT or NFT. The form of existence is chosen by the holder. It is an experiment in NFT fragmentation.

Through the function of bidirectional conversion between NFT and FT, the problem of insufficient liquidity of traditional NFT is solved. Traditional NFT can only be traded through pending orders on the secondary market. It cannot be traded at any time through the liquidity pool like ghostwriting. Buying and selling the tokens in hand is undoubtedly a huge milestone for traditional NFT.
